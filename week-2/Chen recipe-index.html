Avocado Toast

Hi everyone this is Andrew's steps to make an awesome avocado toast breadfast.

What you'll need

Ingredients Lists

Serve 1-2

  • 1/2 avocado
  • 2-4 eggs
  • 2-4 slices of bread
  • 4-8 slices of bacon
  • Salt, Black pepper, Batter

Directions:

  1. Mash the avocado with a fork in a shallow bowl with some salt, black pepper and batter
  2. Slice about 1 tbsp of batter in the pan
  3. When batter melt, crack eggs into pan or whisk for personal preference
  4. Cook bacon and set aside cooling
  5. Toast the bread until browned and crisp
  6. Divide the mashed avocado evenly among the toasts
  7. Top avocado toast with egg and bacon
